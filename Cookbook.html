A cookbook or cookery book is a kitchen reference containing recipes.
Cookbooks may be general, or may specialize in a particular cuisine or category of food.
Recipes in cookbook are organized in various ways: by course (appetizer, first course, main
course, dessert), by main ingredient, by cooking technique, alphabetically, by region or country,
and so on. The main include ilustrations of finished dishes and prepartion steps; discussions of
cooking techniques, advice or kitchen equipment, ingredients, and substitutions; historical and
cultural notes; and so on.

Easy Sugar Cookies

TLT-Sandwich

Quick and easy sugar cookies! They are really good, plain or with candies
in them. My friend uses chocolate mints on top, and they're great!

Ingredients

  1. 2 3/4 all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. 1/2teaspod baking powder
  4. 1 cup butter, softened
  5. 11/2cups white sugar
  6. 1 egg
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Guacamole

Guacamole

You can make this avocado salad smooth or chunky depending on your
tastes.

Ingredients

  1. 3 avocados - pelled, pitted, and mashed
  2. 1 lime, juiced
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  5. 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  6. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  7. 1 pinch ground cayenne papper (Optional)
